About

Cafet is a canteen management system designed to assist in the management and operation of a school or workplace cafeteria. Cafet enables proper menu planning, inventory management, and financial reporting. Cafet also include modules for online ordering, payment processing, and customer management, which can help streamline the cafeteria experience for both employees and customers.
